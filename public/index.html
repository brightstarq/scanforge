Deep Dive

What makes each tool different

Three tools, each with genuine depth. Here's what's under the hood.

🖨

PDF Scanner

Physics-based optical simulation — not a filter

3 pages free · $5 unlimited

Crisp Text Preservation

Grain applies only to bright background pixels — ink stays clean. Letters remain razor sharp at any zoom level.

Channel Misregistration

CCD-bar scanners read R, G, and B in separate passes. Sub-millimetre vibration offsets them, producing the characteristic colour fringing on text edges.

Auto Deskew

Projection-profile algorithm detects and corrects tilt up to ±10°, simulating real-world scanner misalignment convincingly.

Advanced

Back Bleed / Show-through

Real paper is semi-translucent. Each page bleeds a faint ghost of its reverse side — a detail no other tool simulates. Needs 2+ pages to activate.

Advanced

Focus Gradient

Depth-of-field blur that increases away from a chosen edge — simulating a document that wasn't fully flat on the glass. Configurable edge and intensity.

100% Private Processing

Everything runs on your machine locally. Files are never uploaded, never stored. Close the tab and nothing remains.

File Compressor

PDF, images, Word docs, ZIP — all in one tool

Always free · No limits

PDF — Structure-Aware Compression

Our compression engine understands the PDF object model — recompresses embedded images while leaving vector paths, fonts, and text streams untouched. Output stays selectable and searchable. Also handles DOCX, XLSX, PPTX by recompressing their embedded images, and repacks ZIPs at maximum deflate.

Quality Controls Per File Type

PDFs: High Quality (150 DPI), Medium (100 DPI bicubic), and Small File (smart rasterization). Images: quality slider from 20–100% with format conversion to JPEG, PNG, or WEBP. Office files and ZIPs: max DEFLATE repack with embedded image recompression.
