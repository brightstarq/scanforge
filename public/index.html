Crisp Text Preservation

Grain applies only to bright background pixels — ink stays clean. Letters remain razor sharp at any zoom level, always.

02

Completely Private

100% local. Your files never leave your machine. Nothing stored on any server, ever. Close the tab and it's gone.

03

Auto Deskew

Projection-profile algorithm detects and corrects tilt up to ±10°, simulating real-world scanner misalignment.

04

Lossless PNG Output

Pages embed as PNG, not JPEG — zero DCT artifacts on text edges. True A4 dimensions, 300 DPI default.

Process

Three steps to a scanned document

1

Upload your file

Drop any PDF or image — JPG, PNG, WEBP, TIFF, BMP — up to 50 MB. Multi-page PDFs are handled page by page in parallel.

2

Choose your preset

Pick Light, Medium, or Heavy — or open the advanced panel to fine-tune grain, tint, brightness, contrast, rotation, and more.

3

Download instantly

Receive a PDF, original image, or ZIP of PNGs — with warmth, grain, vignette, and sensor jitter baked in.
